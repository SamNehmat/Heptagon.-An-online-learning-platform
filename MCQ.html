Multiple Choice Questions - Help

The Multiple Choice Questions feature allows the user to create question-based quizzes. Questions can have between two and five different options to choose the answer from. Feedback and scoring are also included. The feature includes automatic scoring and will tell the student how many questions they answered correctly out of all the questions in the quiz on the results page. When creating the quiz, you must enter a title for the whole quiz and can create multiple questions. After typing in the question, you can enter between two and five different possibilities of what the answer could be. Ticking the radio button on the right determines which answer is correct. You can also enter feedback for the question that will be displayed once an answer has been selected. Upon creating the quiz, you can save the quiz and share it with other users. If you want to try the quiz out as a user, then click on the Preview button or save it online and open it on your Dashboard. This allows you to test it out and see how the finished quiz will run.
