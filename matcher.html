Matcher - Help

The Matcher feature creates matching exercises. A list of fixed items appears on the left, with jumbled items on the right in the form of a drop-down menu. The user can click on the drop-down menu to select from the right-hand side of the pair to match the left side. This can be used for matching vocabulary to synonyms or translations, or for ordering sentences to form a sequence or a conversation. The feature includes automatic scoring and will tell the student how many pairings they assembled correctly out of all the questions in the exercise on the results page. To create a matcher exercise, enter a title and question. Enter a pair of words in the left and right boxes. Repeat this process again for a full exercise. If extra pairs are needed, click on the Add Pair button and another pair of boxes will appear under the last one. You can also remove the last pair by clicking on the Remove Pair button. You can add multiple questions. Upon creating the quiz, you can save the quiz and share it with other users. If you want to try the quiz out as a user, then click on the Preview button or save it online and open it on your Dashboard. This allows you to test it out and see how the finished quiz will run.
